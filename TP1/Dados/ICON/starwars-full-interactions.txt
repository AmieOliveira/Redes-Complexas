Star Wars coappearances
Networks of scene coappearances of characters in Star Wars, Episodes 1-7 individually and all together. Nodes are unique characters and edges weights denote the number of scenes in which both characters speak.

Citation: E. Gabasova, "The Star Wars social network." Evelina Gabasova's Blog, Accessed 31 December (2015)
